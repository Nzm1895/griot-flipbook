The Griot of Rising Horn

Celebrating 25 years of beautiful life

Author’s Note

This story is a love letter to my brother, Sadiki Hassan Geoffrey Muhammad.
A myth, a memory, a mirror—crafted to honor your 25 years of becoming.
May you always walk in the fire of your name, the laughter of Grandma,
and the presence of every ancestor who whispered your path before you could speak it.
With deepest pride, love, and reverence—
Your sister, The One Who Bears Witness,
Ngozi
